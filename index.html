The random generator generator

Hello human!

This tool helps you create generative grammars.

You can use it to make funny sentences, semi-sensical stories, abstract poetry, idea generators or whatever floats your boat :)

Start by jotting down an example of the thing you'd like to generate

Then think about the structure of that thing and wrap the variable bits with #

Then play around with the editor to add / remove / expand / tweak your grammar variables.

Your generative things will appear in the box below! Click the button to generate more.

What next? Download your grammar as a JSON file, take it to the next level with the Tracery visual editor, or turn it into a Twitter bot with Cheap Bots, Done Quick!

This tool stands on the shoulders of Tracery, JSONeditor and FileSaver.js.
